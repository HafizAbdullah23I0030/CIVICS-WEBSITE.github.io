Mitigating Child Illiteracy Through Technology

Cultural barriers, poverty, and lack of resources are some of the factors contributing to child illiteracy in Pakistan. Many children, particularly in rural and underprivileged areas, lack access to quality formal education. Our project, “Technology as a Means to Mitigate the Causes of Child Illiteracy in Pakistan”, seeks to address this issue by leveraging digital platforms, mobile applications, and e-learning resources.

By utilizing modern technology, we aim to provide accessible, flexible, and cost-effective learning opportunities for children in underserved areas, bridging educational gaps and opening pathways to learning. This initiative also aligns with Sustainable Development Goal 4, which seeks to ensure inclusive and equitable quality education for all. Through our efforts, we hope to bring education to the deprived and empower communities for a brighter future.
